About Me

Muhammad Imran is a Postdoctoral Fellow at Shenzhen Municipal Key Laboratory of AIoT Communications, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. He received his Ph.D. in Software and Communication Engineering from Hongik University, South Korea (2021–2024), M.S. in Computer Science from Virtual University of Pakistan (2019), and B.S./MCS in Computer Science from COMSATS University and BZU, Pakistan.

Before his doctoral studies, he served as an Educator with the School Education Department (SED) Punjab, Pakistan (2016–2021). His research interests include IoT, edge/cloud computing, Information-Centric Networking (ICN/NDN), 5G/6G communication, AI-centric intelligent networking, reinforcement learning, and resource allocation. He received the Best Researcher Award for 2024–2025 and 2022–2023.
