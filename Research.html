My research focuses on advancing future networking technologies through intelligent, efficient, and scalable architectures. Key areas include:

  • Information-Centric Networking (ICN/NDN): Designing microservice-centric forwarding, interest aggregation, and in-network computing for 6G and IoT environments.
  • AI/ML-Centric Intelligent Networking: Reinforcement learning-driven strategies, federated learning for security, and explainable AI in autonomous vehicular systems.
  • Edge/Cloud Computing & Resource Management: Efficient offloading, sharing communication/computation resources, and live VM migration in cloud data centers.
  • 5G/6G and Autonomous Vehicular Communication: QoS-aware forwarding, intrusion detection, and protocol design for V2X and aerial networks.
  • Security & Privacy: Low-rate DDoS detection, ensemble/gossip learning frameworks for intrusion detection.

Ongoing work explores integration of AI with ICN for proactive caching, dependency-aware microservices, and sustainable network resource allocation.
